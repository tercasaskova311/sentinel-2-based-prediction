The official Šumava National Park 2024 annual report:
Logging Activity - 2024: 

Total harvest: 202,205 m³, broken down as:
- Incidental harvest: 166,700 m³ = 82%
- of which bark beetle salvage: 129,613 m³
- of which wind damage: 37,087 m³ (22% of incidental)

- Planned thinning (úmyslné výchovné těžby): 35,505 m³ = 17%
- Self-harvesting (samovýroba): 12 m³
- Bark beetle trap trees felled (lapáky): 101 m³

- Of the processed timber, 28,525 m³ was left in the stands to decay - written off from inventory and left for ecological purposes.

Year	Bark beetle (m³)	Wind (m³)
2024	129,613	            37,087

* Key observation: 2019 was the peak bark beetle year (209,234 m³). 2022–2023 saw a second peak. 2024 shows significant decline - 54% of 2023. Wind damage peaked in 2018 (203,805 m³ - storms).
