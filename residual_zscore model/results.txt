### Labelling Protocol
For each validation point, toggle the 2024 vs 2023 (or 2025 vs 2024) S2 composites and assign one label:

| Label        | Criterion 
| **TP**       | Canopy present in prior year, gone in detection year 
| **FP**       | Canopy present in both years — false alarm 
| **FP_cloud** | Cloud or shadow artefact 
| **TN**       | Intact forest in both years — correct non-detection 
| **FN**       | Obvious damage present but not flagged 

> Canopy gone already in the prior year → expected miss (captured by already-degraded mask, excluded by design).
